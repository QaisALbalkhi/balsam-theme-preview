Classic Hummus with Balsam Tahini

By Nour Al-Hassan·May 1, 2024·5 min read

The secret to truly exceptional hummus? It starts with your tahini. A rich, nutty, well-emulsified tahini is the backbone of every great batch — and that's where Balsam comes in.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (400g) chickpeas, drained and rinsed
  • 3 tablespoons Balsam Tahini
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt to taste

Method

Add chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice and garlic to a food processor. Blend for 3–4 minutes, scraping down the sides as needed. Add ice-cold water one tablespoon at a time until you reach your desired consistency.

Season with salt, transfer to a serving bowl, drizzle with olive oil, and finish with a sprinkle of sumac and fresh parsley.

"The quality of your tahini determines the quality of your hummus. Don't compromise on the star ingredient."
